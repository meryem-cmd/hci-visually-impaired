🌍 About Our Mission

Our project is dedicated to creating an inclusive, accessible, and user-friendly website for people with visual impairments. By following modern web standards and accessibility practices, we aim to break down digital barriers and ensure equal opportunities for everyone.

💡 Why Accessibility Matters

  • 👁️ Over 285 million people worldwide live with visual impairments.
  • 📚 Accessibility ensures equal opportunities for education and participation.
  • ⚖️ In many countries, web accessibility is a legal requirement.

🔑 Core Features of Our Website

Here are the features designed to make your experience smoother:

🌟 Feature ✨ Benefit
⌨️ Keyboard Navigation Full access with Tab, Shift+Tab, and custom shortcuts.
🔊 Screen Reader Support Optimized with semantic HTML and ARIA attributes for better reading flow.
🎨 Customizable Display Switch between dark mode, high contrast, and adjustable text size.
🗣️ Text-to-Speech Listen to the entire website content for hands-free interaction.
